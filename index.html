About PrepBench

PrepBench is a benchmark suite for evaluating large language models on realistic, end-to-end XXX reasoning workflows, from problem understanding to final execution.

The benchmark covers diverse domains and task formats, standardizes evaluation metrics, and provides open-source tooling so that different research groups can compare models fairly and reproducibly.
